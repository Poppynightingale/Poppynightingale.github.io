Hello everyone, and a massive, warm welcome to my brand new website!

Over the coming weeks and months, I plan to share:

  • Cover Reveal Countdown: Be among the first to see the cover!
  • Launch Updates & Pre-order Info: All the details as we get closer to the big day.
  • Bookish Chats & Inspirations: Let's talk about our favourite reads, writing, and everything that sparks creativity!

I truly believe that stories connect us, and I can't wait to connect with all of you. This is the very beginning, and I'd be honoured to have you join me on this adventure.


Let the countdown begin!
